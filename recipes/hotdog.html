Lasagna

A classic American snack, hot dogs are savory, juicy, and flavorful, typically served on a bun with condiments, relishes, and toppings, offering a satisfying and comforting meal or snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound lean pork
  • 3/4 pound lean beef
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Paprika
  • Other spices and flavorings

Steps to Make:

  1. Grind the Meat: Grind the pork, beef, and pork fat through a fine blade of a meat grinder.
  2. Combine the Filling: Blend the ground meat with salt, garlic, paprika, and other spices.
  3. Prepare the Casing: Soak the sheep casings in water for at least an hour.
  4. Stuff the Casing: Fill the casing with the meat mixture and tie off the end.
  5. Cook the Hot Dog: Cook the hot dogs by boiling, pan-frying, broiling, or grilling
