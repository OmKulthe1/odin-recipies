Butter Naan

A classic Indian flatbread, butter naan is soft, fluffy, and flavorful, typically served with a variety of curries, chutneys, and other accompaniments, offering a satisfying and comforting meal or snack.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup lukewarm water
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Steps to Make:

  1. Mix the Dough: Combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a bowl. Gradually add lukewarm water and mix until a smooth dough forms.
  2. Knead the Dough: Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. Let it Rise: Let the dough rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.
  4. Shape the Naan: Divide the dough into 6-8 portions and shape each portion into a ball. Roll out each ball into a thin circle.
  5. Cook the Naan: Cook the naan in a hot skillet or tava with a little oil or butter until it puffs up and the edges start to curl.
